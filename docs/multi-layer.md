30-Layer Encryption & 9-Step Authentication: Overwhelming Depth
While traditional tools like VeraCrypt offer a single layer of defense, bigvirtue1 implements an unprecedented 30-layer encryption engine. Complemented by a 9-step authentication process involving visual photo/drag combinations, it ensures that even if a sub-layer is compromised, the integrity of the core remains absolute. This masterpiece is the result of 5 years of relentless development and thousands of rigorous stress tests.
